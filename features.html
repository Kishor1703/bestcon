Features

Bestcon's AAC (Autoclaved Aerated Concrete) blocks are considered "eco-green" due to their environmentally friendly features like being made from recycled materials (such as fly ash), having a low carbon footprint during production, excellent thermal insulation properties which reduce energy consumption, and being lightweight and easy to transport. This minimizes construction waste and transportation emissions, making them a sustainable building material choice.
