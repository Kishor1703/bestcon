AAC BLOCKS

AAC Blocks (Autoclaved Aerated Concrete) is a certified green building material. These precast concrete blocks are extremely lightweight and widely used for internal and external construction. We are ISO 9001-2015 certified company and registered with GRIHA and IGBC. We manufacture AAC Blocks with IS:2185 Part 3(1984) standard with a capacity of 1000 cubic meters per day. The blocks are easy to install, nail, drill and cut. We use cement, sand, fly ash, gypsum, lime, and water.

Joint Mortar

BESTCON Jointing Mortar gives excellent bonding between two AAC blocks. It is suitable for bonding most construction materials, both indoors and outdoors. It provides high bonding due to its high tensile adhesion strength. Designed for laying AAC blocks, Fly ash bricks, and more.
