Why AAC block?

The environment where we live is being vandalized due to the climatic change behaviors. AAC block is a gift to the world where the need of the hour is met with. They are lighter in weight, stronger in compressive strength and a massive cost saver. They are contributing to the existence of world by reduction of carbon foot prints and through various advantages they help the world breathe-easy. These blocks are excellent in thermal insulation properties, highly Energy efficient, environmental friendly, reliable, durable and recyclable.

How do I choose the right AAC block for my project?

• Project Requirements: Choose based on whether the blocks will be used for load-bearing or non-load-bearing walls.
• Size and Shape: Depending on the project, you may choose standard blocks, panels, or slabs.
• Climate Considerations: Ensure the right type of AAC block is chosen for the local climate (e.g., with additional moisture protection if necessary).
